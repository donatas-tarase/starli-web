Privacy and boundaries

Personal reflection, not public performance.

Starli is an individual reflection tool. It is not a social network, does not ask your partner to participate, and should not be treated as therapy, counseling, medical advice, or emergency support.

  • No public feed. Starli is for your own private reflection practice.
  • No partner required. You can reflect on your own after a conversation.
  • Clear legal pages. Read the Privacy Policy and Terms for the formal details.
  • Professional support still matters. If you need urgent help or professional care, contact a qualified professional or local emergency service.

FAQ

Questions people usually ask first.

What is Starli?

Starli is a private iOS reflection app for thinking through relationship moments, noticing communication patterns, and building a calmer reflection habit.

Do I need my partner to use it?

No. Starli is designed as an individual reflection tool. You can use it on your own after meaningful or difficult conversations.

Is Starli therapy?

No. Starli is not therapy, counseling, medical treatment, diagnosis, crisis support, or a replacement for professional help.

Is my reflection public?

No. Starli is not a social network and is not built around public posting or a public feed.

Which languages are available?

Starli supports English and Lithuanian.

Which devices are supported?

Starli is available through the App Store for iPhone. The App Store listing notes iOS 15.1 or later compatibility.

How do I download the app?
