Product direction

A quieter alternative to noisy self-improvement apps.

Starli is being built as an individual app, not a social network. The focus is personal reflection, daily awareness, and small behavior changes that compound over time.

No public feed

Your reflections are for you, not for public performance.

Practical prompts

Questions are designed to help users think clearly about real relationship moments.

Gentle consistency

Daily reminders and simple routines help users return without pressure.

Not professional advice

Starli is a reflection tool and is not a substitute for therapy, counseling, or medical support.
