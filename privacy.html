Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Introduction

Starli is a relationship reflection app. We collect and process personal information responsibly and only as needed to provide and improve the service.

Information We Collect

We may collect your name and email address for account registration, reflection entries you create in the app, and usage statistics that help us understand how the app is used.

How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and maintain the Starli service.
  • To improve user experience, performance, and product quality.
  • We do not share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored using encryption and protected with industry-standard security practices designed to prevent unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

Your Rights

You may request an export or deletion of your data at any time. To make a request, please contact us through the Contact page or email support@starli.app.

Third-Party Services

Starli may rely on third-party services such as Apple platform services and analytics providers. These services operate under their own privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

Starli is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will provide notice through the app or website.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, visit our Contact page or write to support@starli.app.
