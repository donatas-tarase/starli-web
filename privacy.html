2. Personal data we collect

Depending on how you interact with us, we may collect the following categories of personal data:

  • Contact details, such as your name, email address, company name, and message content.
  • Business relationship information, such as product interest, support history, billing-related details, and communication records.
  • Website usage and technical data, such as IP address, browser type, device type, operating system, pages visited, referring pages, timestamps, and security logs.
  • Starli app account and usage data, where applicable, such as account details, reflection content you choose to create, settings, reminders, and product usage data needed to operate the app.

3. How we collect personal data

We collect personal data directly from you, automatically through our website or apps, and from service providers that help us operate our products and business.

4. How we use personal data

We use personal data to:

  • Operate, maintain, and improve our website and products.
  • Respond to inquiries, support requests, and privacy requests.
  • Manage customer, partner, vendor, and business relationships.
  • Provide product functionality, user accounts, settings, reminders, and support.
  • Analyze performance, troubleshoot issues, and improve user experience.
  • Protect our website, products, users, and business from fraud, spam, abuse, and unauthorized access.
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect our legal rights.
  • Send service-related messages and, where permitted, product or marketing updates.

5. Legal bases for processing

Where applicable data protection laws require a legal basis, we process personal data based on one or more of the following:

  • Contract, where processing is needed to provide a product or service.
  • Legitimate interests, such as operating, securing, improving, and promoting our business.
  • Consent, where we ask for consent for specific processing, such as certain cookies or marketing communications.
  • Legal obligation, where processing is needed to comply with applicable law.
  • Legal claims, where processing is needed to establish, exercise, or defend our rights.

6. Cookies and similar technologies

Our website may use cookies, local storage, and similar technologies for essential functionality, security, analytics, and performance. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some website functionality.

7. Starli app data handling

When you use the Starli iOS app, reflection text, account settings, relationship labels you create, reminders, and related app data may be stored in cloud systems used to operate the product. These reflections are not public and are not shown in a social feed.

If you choose to use voice input, audio data may be processed to create a transcript. Reflection text, transcripts, and related context may also be processed by AI service providers so Starli can generate app features such as responses, summaries, embeddings, relationship dynamics, and suggested next steps.

You can request deletion of your account and associated app data through the app where available or by contacting us at privacy@starli.app. Some limited records may be retained where required for security, legal, billing, or operational reasons.

8. Sharing personal data

We do not sell personal data. We may share personal data with service providers and partners who help us operate our website, apps, and business, including hosting providers, analytics providers, email and communication tools, customer support tools, billing providers, security providers, and professional advisors. We may also disclose personal data where required by law or in connection with a merger, acquisition, reorganization, or sale of assets.

9. International transfers

Starli Labs is based in Lithuania, but our service providers may operate in other countries. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards for international transfers, such as contractual protections and legally recognized transfer mechanisms.

10. Data retention

We retain personal data only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer period is required or permitted by law. Contact, support, and business relationship records may be retained for a reasonable period after our last interaction. Technical and security logs are retained for a limited period unless needed for security, troubleshooting, or legal reasons.

11. Security

We use technical, organizational, and administrative measures designed to protect personal data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. No system or method of transmission is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

12. Your privacy rights

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to the processing of your personal data, request data portability, withdraw consent, and lodge a complaint with a competent data protection authority.

To exercise your rights, contact us at privacy@starli.app. We may need to verify your identity before responding.

13. Children

Our website and products are not directed to children under 16 or under the age required by applicable law. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe we have collected such data, please contact us.

14. Third-party links

Our website and products may contain links to third-party websites, platforms, or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those third parties.

15. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new “Last updated” date.
