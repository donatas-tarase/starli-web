Digital Downloads

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Starli Labs, MB (“Starli”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects personal data when merchants install or use the Digital Downloads app (the “App”), and when customers of those merchants receive or access digital product downloads through the App.

Starli Labs, MB is registered in Lithuania. Company code: 307633666. Registered address: Savanorių pr. 407-34, LT-49361 Kaunas, Lithuania. Website: starli.app. Privacy contact: privacy@starli.app. Support contact: support@starli.app.

1. Roles and responsibility

The App is built for merchants who use it to attach digital files to store products and variants, send download links after paid orders, manage customer access, and track download activity.

For personal data relating to a merchant’s customers, orders, products, and download access, the merchant is generally the data controller or business, and Starli acts as a data processor or service provider on behalf of that merchant. In this role, we process data according to the merchant’s instructions, our agreement with the merchant, and applicable law.

For personal data relating to merchant accounts, app administration, billing metadata, support communications, website visitors, and our own business operations, Starli may act as a data controller.

If you are a customer of a merchant using the App and you have questions about your order, download, or personal data, please contact the merchant directly. We will assist the merchant where required.

2. Personal data we collect

2.1 Merchant and account data

  • Name, email address, business name, store name, website, and contact details.
  • App installation data, site identifiers, instance identifiers, account identifiers, and configuration settings.
  • Subscription plan, billing status, and payment-related metadata received through the relevant app marketplace or billing provider.
  • Support messages, feedback, onboarding information, and communications with us.
  • App usage data, such as dashboard activity, settings changes, feature usage, and error logs.

2.2 Store, product, file, and order data

  • Product and variant identifiers, product names, SKU or similar product metadata.
  • Digital file names, file references, file sizes, file types, storage references, and file access settings.
  • Order identifiers, order numbers, purchased products, payment status, order status, and timestamps.
  • Customer name, email address, contact ID, and other order information needed to deliver digital files or provide support.
  • Delivery email status, resend history, access status, download limits, expiry dates, and revoked or restored access records.

We do not intentionally collect full payment card numbers through the App.

2.3 Download activity and technical data

  • Download timestamp, download count, access status, and related order or file identifier.
  • IP address, browser type, device type, operating system, user agent, and similar technical data where needed for security, troubleshooting, fraud prevention, abuse prevention, or analytics.
  • Link expiry, access attempts, failed access events, and delivery errors.

2.4 Website and communication data

  • Name, email address, company name, and message content.
  • Newsletter or marketing preferences, if applicable.
  • IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited, referring pages, and cookie-related data.
  • Support, demo, sales, or feedback communications.

3. How we collect personal data

We collect personal data directly from merchants, from the connected store platform and its APIs, from customers when they access download links or interact with delivery emails, from communications with us, automatically through app logs and server logs, and from third-party service providers that help us operate the App.

4. How we use personal data

We use personal data to provide, operate, and maintain the App; connect digital files to products and variants; generate and send download links after paid orders; manage download limits, expiry dates, customer access, and delivery history; display digital orders and download activity; resend delivery emails; provide support; troubleshoot issues; monitor performance; protect security; prevent fraud, misuse, unauthorized access, and abuse; manage subscriptions and billing metadata; send service-related messages; send marketing communications where permitted; comply with legal obligations; and enforce our rights.

5. Legal bases for processing

Where Starli acts as a processor or service provider, we process personal data on behalf of the merchant and according to the merchant’s instructions.

Where Starli acts as a controller, we process personal data based on contract, legitimate interests, consent where required, legal obligation, and the establishment, exercise, or defense of legal claims.

6. Sharing personal data

We do not sell personal data. We may share personal data with the merchant that installed and uses the App, store platform providers and APIs, hosting and cloud storage providers, email delivery providers, billing and app marketplace providers, analytics and logging providers, customer support tools, security providers, professional advisors, public authorities where required by law, and a successor entity in the event of a merger, acquisition, financing, reorganization, or sale of assets.

Our service providers are authorized to use personal data only as needed to provide services to us and are required to protect personal data appropriately.

7. Merchant files and customer content

The App allows merchants to upload or connect files for digital product delivery. Merchants are responsible for the files they upload, the rights to distribute those files, and ensuring that files do not contain unnecessary personal data or unlawful content.

The App is not intended for storing sensitive personal data. Merchants should not upload files containing sensitive personal data unless they have a lawful basis and have implemented appropriate safeguards.

8. Cookies and similar technologies

Our website and App may use cookies, pixels, local storage, and similar technologies for essential functionality, security, analytics, and performance. Essential cookies or similar technologies may be required for login, security, app operation, and fraud prevention. Analytics or marketing cookies will be used only where permitted by applicable law and, where required, with consent.

The connected store platform may also provide cookie consent tools or visitor consent settings. Merchants are responsible for configuring their store’s cookie banner and privacy notices where required by applicable law.

9. Data retention

We retain personal data only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. Merchant account and app configuration data is retained while the App is installed or the merchant account is active. Order, file delivery, and download access records are retained as needed to provide the App, support merchants, maintain access history, and resolve delivery issues. Support communications may be retained for support, service improvement, and legal purposes. Security logs, technical logs, and error logs are retained for a limited period unless needed for security, fraud prevention, debugging, or legal reasons. Backups may retain data for a limited backup cycle before deletion.

After uninstalling the App, we may delete or anonymize app data within a reasonable period, unless we need to retain it to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, prevent fraud or abuse, maintain security, or enforce agreements.

10. International transfers

Starli is based in Lithuania, but we may use service providers located in other countries. Personal data may be stored or processed in the European Economic Area, the United States, or other countries where we or our service providers operate. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards for international transfers, such as contractual protections, data processing agreements, and other legally recognized transfer mechanisms.

11. Security

We use technical, organizational, and administrative measures designed to protect personal data, including access controls, encryption in transit, secure infrastructure, monitoring, and limited access based on business need. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

12. Data subject rights

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, restriction, portability, withdrawal of consent, objection to processing, or to lodge a complaint with a competent data protection authority.

If you are a customer of a merchant using the App, please contact the merchant directly regarding your personal data. We process customer and order data on behalf of the merchant and will assist the merchant as required.

If you are a merchant, website visitor, or other individual whose data is processed by Starli as a controller, contact us at privacy@starli.app. We may need to verify your identity before responding.

13. Marketing communications

We may send merchants service-related messages, product updates, onboarding messages, and administrative notices. Where we send promotional emails, you can unsubscribe using the instructions in the email or by contacting us.

14. Children

The App is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 16 or under the age required by applicable law. If you believe that we have collected such data, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

15. Third-party links and services

The App, delivery emails, or our website may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third parties.

16. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on our website with a new “Last updated” date. If we make material changes, we may notify merchants through the App, email, or another reasonable method.
